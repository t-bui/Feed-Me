About me

Feed Me is a website that is designed to help UVA students find free food on grounds. Organizations often give out free food, and Feed Me provides a central location for students to find these opportunities to eat free meals. Organizations or other students that know about the free food and its time and location will have the ability to post information about the food along with an expiration time when it should be removed from the site.
